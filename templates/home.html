About EbFit

Ebfit is designed to be free, simple, and student-focused. Instead of confusing users with a wide variety of categories and expensive subscriptions, Ebfit allows students to create an account and edit posts, request and join activities, as well as track activities. This positions Ebfit as a user-friendly website that helps MMU students find reliable sports activities.
