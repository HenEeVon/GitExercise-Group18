About us

ebFit is designed to be free, simple, and student-focused. Instead of confusing users with a wide variety of categories and expensive subscription, ebFit allows students to create an account and edit posts, request and join activities that locate within 4.5km distance from MMU Indoor Sports Complex. This positions ebFit as a user-friendly website that helps students to find reliable sports activities.
