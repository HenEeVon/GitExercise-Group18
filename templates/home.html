About us

ebFit is designed to be free, simple, and student-focused. Instead of confusing users with a wide variety of categories and expensive subscription, ebFit allows students to create an account and edit posts, request and join activities, as well as track activities. This positions Ebfit as a user-friendly website that helps students to find reliable sports activities.
